FTA Committees

Jump to a Committee:

Rules & Elections | Special Education | Community Outreach | Human Rights | Retirement | Political Action | IPD | Membership/Social | Organizing/Communications

Rules and Elections:

This committee is in charge of the election process for our election at the association. This year, they are looking to read through the by-laws for elections and make any needed updates. They also need people to help count ballots come election time.


Chairperson: Jill Schuster

Special Education:

This committee explores the continued concerns with special education. The ever changing special education process provides continued challenges.

Chairpersons: Troy Liggins and Melissa Yamarone

Community Outreach:

This committee reaches out to the community of Fontana and offers a chance to give back. This may be in the form of giving senior citizens something they really need or handing out toy filled stocking at Christmas time.

Chairperson: Rosanne Gray

Human Rights:

This committee is in charge of the student contest offered through CTA. The committee needs help organizing entries, judging entries, and notifying winners. There are also plans to work with the district this year to create a multicultural fair.

Chairperson: Janet Nasir

Retirement:

This committee helps those close to retirement get information about benefits, STRS, and other areas. This committee also looks at ways to honor those retiring each year.

Chairpersons: Christina Halverson and Steve Jennings

Political Action:

This committee works with school board elections. Although there is no election this year, we have a big election next fall and need to begin to get ready for it.

Chairpersons: Jennifer Norton and Margaret Vollmer

IPD:

This committee plans professional development opportunities at the association. The committee works with CTA in deciding what is offered.

Chairperson: Janice Taylor

Membership/Social:

This committee does so many different things. It helps reach out to our new teachers. The committee looks for ways to make members feel appreciated – from t-shirts to lunch bags. This committee also sets up all the TGIFs.

Chairperson: Colleen Gerke

Organizing/Communications

This year, the focus of this committee is to improve communications. The committee will be looking at setting up social media accounts – Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, and others. The committee needs people to be involved in all aspects, including monitoring the sites.

Chairperson: Open
